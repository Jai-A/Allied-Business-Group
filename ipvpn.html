Enterprise MPLS Services

AlliedBG's IP VPN Service provides a complete, fully-managed MPLS solution that is secure, reliable and cost effective. National coverage can service multiple business locations. Connections are provided over dedicated carrier services, securely transferring your confidential business information.

Flexible Connectivity

IP VPN delivers a high performance, flexible solution by sourcing the appropriate connectivity solution to meet your requirements. Business sites can be seamlessly connected using a mix of fibre, fixed wireless, NBN, Ethernet over Copper or 3G/4G services, dependent on cost and performance objectives.

Customer Management

Customer site status and service performance can be simply and easily monitored on a 24x7 basis, enabling transparent and proactive management of availability and performance.
